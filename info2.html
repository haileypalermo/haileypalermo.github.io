There are many nationally talented male and female volleyball players known worldwide. Brenda Castillo, a libero from the Dominican Republic, has won many awards for her skills playing on the national level at only 16 years old. She was given the nickname "El picollo grande libero", meaning little giant. However, she faced many challenges and rumors at one point during her career. As a result, Brenda was temporarily separated from the national team due to alledged indiscipline reasons in the year of 2013.

Similarly, a male volleyball player known as Maksim Mikhaylov has won over five gold medals at 32 years old. He has won major international tournaments with the Russian national team as well with his club team called Zenit Kazan. Mikhaylov believes that volleyball has grown due to the promotion of the sport as well as television broadcasts. In addition to his may gold medals, Mikhaylov won the Champions League at his home court in Russia. He claimed it was the most memorable moment of his entire career.

Finally, from the United States, Karch Kiraly is said to be the best volleyball player of all time. He attended UCLA, leading them to a record of 123-5 as well as three NCAA national championships. Originally, Kiraly was supposed to become a doctor, but after he graduated, he joined the U.S. national team. He helped them win gold medals in both the 1984 Olympic Games as well as the 1988 Olympic Games.
